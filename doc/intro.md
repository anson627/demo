## What does your typical day look like?

I like to describe my role with three hats:

**Agent Orchestrator** — I spend a good chunk of my day designing and prompting AI agents to write code, generate documentation, and automate repetitive workflows. I review and test the output thoroughly to ensure quality and maintainability. The goal is to multiply the team's velocity by treating AI as a force multiplier, not just a coding assistant.

**Data Plumber** — A lot of infrastructure work is about getting the right data flowing to the right place. I build benchmarks, collect performance metrics, and prepare data for priority and technical decision-making. I also advocate for perf and scale testing during design reviews so we measure before we ship.

**Incident Firefighter** — When things break, I jump in to diagnose and resolve issues during rollout to protect SLA and retain customers. Outside of incidents, I lead regular operational reviews to identify recurring failures and sub-optimal patterns, enrich the oncall agent skills, so the whole team can respond effectively.
